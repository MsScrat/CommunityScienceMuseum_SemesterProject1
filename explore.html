Meet our robot

Hero is our first self built robot. He is a great walker, teacher and dancer.

He can teach math, biology, physics and do some artwork by your design. Give him a description and watch your beautiful design come alive infont of you.

We would recommend to ask Hero to tell you a joke on you're visit. He is stored with over a thousand jokes.

If you ask him to, maybe he will join you in a dance off where you can win a price.

Hero is great with children and would love to meet your entire family!

Visit the VR world

Our updated VR set allows you to experience viritual reality with mind blowing graphics and no headaces!

We offer five worlds to explore: Time travel, world of politic, what if, moon walker and games of history.

Ever wanted to meet Albert Einstein, ride on dinosaurs, be a part of your favorit game, walk the moons surface or try as president for the entire world?

If you are above the age of 10, you are welcome to visit the world of your choise and explore it for yourself!

Travel through space

Experience technology like never before in our modified auditorium! Listen, see, feel and touch space with a sensitive 4D sensation.

This is a true-to-life experience where you get to explore the possibilities of our universe, the dangers it posses and the impressive technologies which have given us the information we have today.

What could the future bring and how can we further use our universe, want to see for yourself?

The auditorium has no set age limit. Come explore space and it's endless possibilities with family, friends our with your classmates.

The auditorium can also be rented with a guide on weekdays up until three hours for birthdays, school trips, teambulding or just for your friendgroup.

Build the future with LEGO

Visit our Lego section where you can build how the future looks in your eyes! Every build gets to be a part of a contest where we each week award and display the winner, maybe you? There are three separate winner for children, teens and adults, so everyone can partizipate building the future!
